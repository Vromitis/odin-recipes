Tofu scramble

Powerfull and legenderay, this eastern tofu scrumble converts the most opinionated carnis


  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (18 ounce) package firm tofu, crumbled
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley


  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat and cook onion until soft and translucent, about 3 minutes. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add tofu and mushrooms and cook until tofu starts to brown, 8 to 10 minutes. Sir in soy sauce and parsley.
